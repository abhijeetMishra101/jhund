### 3. Usage-Based Pricing Model Adopted
- Pricing model confirmed: **$0.10 per action** after a **free tier of 50 actions**.

---

## Organic Growth Discussion

### The Core Tension
Usage-based pricing creates friction at the free tier limit, which can stall word-of-mouth and organic growth if not managed carefully.

### Strategies Discussed
1. **Make the free tier shareable** — referral loops that reward users with bonus actions
2. **"Powered by" moments** — passive brand discovery through product outputs
3. **Make the free tier feel generous early, then sticky** — ensure users hit their "aha moment" well before the limit
4. **Reduce upgrade anxiety** — spend caps or clear limits to build trust

### Key Risk Identified
Users may **self-censor** (avoid using the product to save actions), which kills habit-building and referrals.

---

## Free Tier Limit Discussion

### Should the limit increase from 50 to 100?
- Suggestion came from **gut feel**, not data.
- Explored a middle ground of **60 actions**.
- **Decision: Keep it at 50 actions** — revisit once real usage data is available to determine whether users are churning before or after their "aha moment."

---

## Next Steps

- Revisit analytics dashboard for a future version (v2+).
